About Me

I grew up in South Florida and went to The Benjamin School K through 12. For college, I spent a year and a half at UNC, then the next two and a half years at a small international business school in Paris, where I earned my BBA and majored in International Business.

I am very traditional and chose to live in France for the slower pace of life. That said, I was always top of my class in computer science and robotics at Benjamin, and now I am completely in love with using Claude and experimenting with new AI tools every day.

My philosophy on education is that it should never stop, and that it does not matter where you get it, as long as you know how to push yourself.

I am against the idea that AI will make people obsolete. I think, at minimum, people will always keep learning as a way to gain confidence and self-actualize.

Now, graduated and living in NYC, I start and run my own companies using AI as my biggest asset. One of my companies even offers a similar service for adults, at a higher price and with fewer hands-on lessons.

I love kids, but for now, I am happy just having a dog. For that reason, I would love to teach kids how to start learning.

If you want to see my professional background, you can find me on LinkedIn. If you want to read more about AI from my perspective, check out my Substack.
